Frequently Asked Questions

For Participants

How do I register for Devnovate?

You can register through our official platform. Simply sign up, choose your role, and follow the steps to complete your registration.

What are the benefits of participating?

Participants gain hands-on experience, mentorship, networking opportunities, and a chance to win prizes and internships.

Do I need to have coding experience?

No! Whether you're a beginner or an expert, Devnovate welcomes everyone to collaborate and create innovative solutions.

Can I work on my own idea?

Yes! You can either work on your own project or choose a challenge provided by Devnovate or sponsors.

What happens after the hackathon?

Winning teams may receive funding, mentorship, or internship opportunities. Many projects continue development beyond Devnovate.

For Sponsors

Why should I sponsor Devnovate?

Sponsoring Devnovate provides brand exposure, access to emerging talent, and direct collaboration on innovative projects.

What sponsorship packages are available?

We offer multiple sponsorship tiers, including branding opportunities, networking access, and the ability to introduce challenge statements.

Can sponsors provide hackathon challenges?

Absolutely! Sponsors can introduce real-world problem statements that participants work on during the event.

How do sponsors interact with participants?

Sponsors can mentor teams, host workshops, and directly network with participants to find top talent.

Are sponsors involved in judging?

Yes! Sponsors can be part of the judging panel, mentor teams, and offer special prizes or job opportunities.
