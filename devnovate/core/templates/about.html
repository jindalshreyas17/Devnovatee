About Devnovate

Devnovate is a dynamic platform bringing together innovators, sponsors, and participants for exciting hackathons and tech events.

  • Seamless role-based access for Admins, Sponsors, and Participants.
  • Opportunities for sponsors to showcase their brands with logo spotlights.
  • Engaging hackathon events with national and regional levels.
  • Real-time project collaboration and team management tools.
  • Secure and user-friendly registration and profile management.
  • Exciting prizes and recognition for top participants.
